Natural Commerce: The order management system for grocery & CPG industry in the US

Natural Commerce is an e-commerce platform that sepcializes in providing access to natural, organic, and specialty products for grocery retailers. It helps to minimize waste and maximize revenue by offering a wide selection of products to consumers through national distributors like UNFI and KeHE. Tech-Chicago built a scalable supplier portal and product mapping system for Natural Commerce, enabling real-time inventory updates and independent order management. We streamlined logistics with dynamic order allocation and real-time tracking. A custom data transformation tool ensured seamless integration across retailer and warehouse systems.

LittlePay: Simplifying Public Transportation Payments

LittlePay is the world’s leading and most trusted provider of payment infrastructure for transit and mobility. Their goal is making contactless bank card acceptance faster, cheaper, and less complex for the public transit sector. We performed a UX and technical audit of the existing platform, identified potential improvements beyond the way it looks. Then, together with the client decided on the action plan: redesigning all the existing flows of the platform with new goals in mind, refactored existing features with the goal of enhancing the user experience. We built several features in both the Merchant’s and Traveler’s portal.

Tech-Chicago’s Web Development Process

Discovery & Planning

It all begins with the Discovery & Planning phase. Now is when we embark on a collaborative adventure, delving deep into the heart of your business objectives and engaging in insightful consultations with you. By understanding your unique vision and the distinct needs of your target audience, we sculpt a strategic plan that not only aligns with your goals but also optimizes them. This plan becomes the compass guiding us toward the creation of a tailored, highly effective web solution that seamlessly blends with your audience’s preferences and exceeds their expectations. It’s a process that transforms your vision into a reality, ensuring your digital presence stands out in a crowded online world.

Design & Development

As we venture further into the Design & Development phase, our talented creative designers take the reins to craft visually captivating designs that are as appealing as they are functional. Simultaneously, our skilled developers breathe life into these designs with clean and efficient code. This dynamic synergy between design and development ensures your website doesn’t just look great; it performs flawlessly. The result? A digital masterpiece that not only catches the eye but also provides a seamless and engaging user experience, driving visitors to take the actions you desire. It’s the art and science of web development working in perfect harmony.

Testing & Quality Assurance

Before we unveil your web creation to the world, our journey takes us through the Testing & Quality Assurance phase. Here, we leave no stone unturned in our quest for perfection. Rigorous testing across various browsers and devices ensures that your web solution stands strong in the face of compatibility challenges. Our dedicated QA team meticulously hunts down and rectifies any issues, ensuring your website’s launch is a resounding success. It’s the quality assurance that guarantees your website not only works seamlessly but also delivers a consistently exceptional experience to your users, building trust and credibility one click at a time.

Launch & Maintenance

With your web solution ready to shine, the Launch & Maintenance phase takes center stage. But our support doesn’t end with the grand unveiling. We stand by your side, helping you launch your creation successfully to the world. However, it doesn’t stop there. Our commitment extends to ongoing maintenance and support, ensuring that your software runs like a well-oiled machine. You can focus on your core business while we handle the technical intricacies, ensuring your online presence remains efficient, secure, and up-to-date. It’s the partnership that ensures your digital journey continues to flourish, evolve, and conquer new horizons.
